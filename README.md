# Privacy Policy for DealList

**Last updated: July 02, 2026**

DealList ("we", "our", or "us") operates the DealList mobile application (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding data collection and storage for our application.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

---

### 1. Information Collection and Use

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or user-identifiable data.** 

* **User-Generated Content:** Any data or content you create within the app, such as shopping lists, favorites, price comparisons, or custom notes, is **stored strictly locally on your own device**. This information is never uploaded to our servers, nor do we have access to it.
* **No Account Required:** You do not need to register an account or provide an email address, phone number, or name to use the full functionality of DealList.

---

### 2. Third-Party Services and Cloud Syncing

To provide you with up-to-date flyer data and supermarket regular prices, our app fetches public information from cloud databases. 

* **Google Firebase / Firestore:** The application communicates with Google Firebase/Firestore to download grocery data. While DealList does not actively collect your personal data, Google Play Services and Firebase may collect automated log data, performance metrics, or basic device identifiers for stability and network synchronization purposes. 
* We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party providers:
  * [Google Play Services Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
  * [Firebase Privacy and Security](https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy)

---

### 3. Data Storage and Security

Because all your personal app data (like shopping lists) resides locally on your device, the security of that data depends on your device's security settings. If you delete the DealList application or clear its data via your device settings, your locally stored shopping lists and preferences will be permanently removed, as we do not keep backups on any server.

---

### 4. Children's Privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal data from any user, children's privacy is fully protected within the app.

---

### 5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
